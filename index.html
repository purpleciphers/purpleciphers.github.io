Welcome to Purple Ciphers

Introduction

Hello and welcome! I build free, web-based simulators of historical cipher machines so people can use them without any coding or technical skills. This site is always in progress and although it's realatively small now the plan is to expand it to include many more ciphers. The name is inspired by my favorite color/cipher machine: PURPLE!

Contents

PURPLE Cipher Machine
a Japanese diplomatic cipher used during World War II. Broken by the US SIS. Also called Angooki Taipu B or Type B Cipher machine.
M209 Converter
an American cipher machine used to send low-level messages in the field. Designed by Boris Hagelin and broken by German intelligence. Also called CSP-1500, AM-1, or C-38.
